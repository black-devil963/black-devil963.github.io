How NiS Works

    Register and create your profile


  • Sign up with Facebook/Gmail/Mobile number or email id
  • Verify your mobile number and email address
  • Complete your profile i.e., present class, income, location, gender and other required fields
  • Download NiS app to keep track of scholarships

    Get notified for matching scholarships


  • Based on your profile you will receive matching scholarship email/sms/whatsapp/app notifications
  • Keep your profile updated to receive relevant notifications, you can unsubscribe if you donâ€™t want to receive notifications anymore

    Apply for scholarships


  • Apply for scholarships with easy to fill form - most of your information will be prefilled
  • Upload high-quality documents with all fields visible
  • Regularly monitor your email/mobile for NiS notifications
  • Respond to questions in telephonic interviews

    Become a scholar


  • If you are selected as a scholar, you will receive notifications from NiS
  • You can also check results on scholarship results page
  • Upload your attested documents on NiS and provide bank information
  • Receive scholarship award directly in your bank account
